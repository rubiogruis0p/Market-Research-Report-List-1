<p><ul><li>Radial Tubeless Tyre</li><li>Bias Tubeless Tyre</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tubeless tyre market is primarily divided into two types: radial tubeless tyres and bias tubeless tyres. Radial tubeless tyres feature a design where the internal cords are arranged radially, enabling better fuel efficiency, increased durability, and improved handling on the road. Conversely, bias tubeless tyres have overlapping layers of fabric, providing a sturdier construction suited for off-road conditions but with a less responsive performance. Both types cater to different vehicle applications, emphasizing safety and performance in diverse environments.</p></p>
