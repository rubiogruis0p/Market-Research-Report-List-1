<p><p>The floor decals market comprises primarily two types: non-slip floor decals and ordinary floor decals. Non-slip floor decals are designed to provide traction and prevent slipping, making them ideal for high-traffic areas or locations prone to spills, enhancing safety. Ordinary floor decals, on the other hand, serve mainly for branding, directional guidance, or promotional purposes without special traction features, often used in retail and event settings. Both types enhance visual communication while catering to different safety and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
