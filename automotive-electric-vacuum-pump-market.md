<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Vacuum Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Type</li><li>Leaf Type</li><li>Swing Piston Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electric vacuum pump market comprises three main types: diaphragm type, leaf type, and swing piston type. Diaphragm pumps utilize a flexible membrane to create vacuum pressure, ideal for smaller applications. Leaf type pumps use a series of vanes to generate vacuum, offering compact designs with moderate performance. Swing piston pumps feature a piston that swings to create a vacuum, known for higher efficiency and power output, making them suitable for demanding applications. Each type serves specific vehicle needs, enhancing performance and efficiency.</p></p>
