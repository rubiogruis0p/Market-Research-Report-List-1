<p><p>The engineered molded wood pallets market features two primary types: nestable pallets and rackable pallets. Nestable pallets are designed to save space when not in use, allowing them to fit seamlessly into each other, making them ideal for storage and transportation. Conversely, rackable pallets are engineered to withstand the weight of goods piled on top of them and are designed for use within warehouse racking systems. Both types offer sustainability and durability, catering to diverse logistics and material handling needs.</p></p>
