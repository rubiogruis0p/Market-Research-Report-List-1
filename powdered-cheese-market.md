<p><ul><li>Pure Cheese Powder</li><li>Cheese Powder Blends</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powdered cheese market comprises two primary types: pure cheese powder and cheese powder blends. Pure cheese powder is derived from a single cheese type, preserving its unique flavor and nutritional profile, making it ideal for gourmet applications. In contrast, cheese powder blends combine various cheese types, often enhancing taste and cost-effectiveness, and are suited for mass-market products like snacks and seasonings. Both segments cater to diverse culinary needs across the food industry, from home cooking to professional kitchens.</p></p>
