<p><ul><li>Sensor Composed of Pressure-sensitive Material</li><li>Traditional Conductor Flexible Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tactile imaging market encompasses various sensor types, primarily categorized into pressure-sensitive material sensors and traditional conductor flexible sensors. Pressure-sensitive material sensors detect and respond to applied force, enabling detailed mapping of touch and pressure distribution. In contrast, traditional conductor flexible sensors utilize conductive materials to measure pressure changes across flexible surfaces, providing essential data for applications in robotics, healthcare, and interactive technology. Both types enhance user experience by enabling more intuitive and responsive touch interfaces in various fields.</p></p>
