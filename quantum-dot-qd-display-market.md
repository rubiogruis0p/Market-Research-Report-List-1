<p><strong>The Quantum Dot QD Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QDEF</li><li>QLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quantum Dot Display market primarily features two types: QDEF (Quantum Dot Enhancement Film) and QLED (Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode). QDEF enhances standard LCD displays by using a film of quantum dots to improve color accuracy and brightness. In contrast, QLED technology incorporates quantum dots within an LED backlight, producing vibrant colors and deeper blacks, delivering superior image quality. Both technologies leverage quantum dots to enhance visual performance, appealing to consumers seeking advanced display solutions.</p></p>
