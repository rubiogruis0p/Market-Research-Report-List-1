<p><strong>The Total Chlorine Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Fluorescence Total Chlorine Analyzers</li><li>UV Fluorescence Total Chlorine Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Total chlorine analyzers facilitate the measurement of total chlorine levels in water, playing a crucial role in water quality monitoring. X-ray fluorescence total chlorine analyzers utilize X-ray technology to detect chlorine content, offering high precision and sensitivity. In contrast, UV fluorescence total chlorine analyzers employ ultraviolet light to excite chlorine molecules, leading to fluorescence that is measured to determine chlorine levels. Both types serve vital functions in industries like water treatment, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes, ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
