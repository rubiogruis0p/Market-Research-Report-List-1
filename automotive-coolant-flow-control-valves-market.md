<p><p>Automotive coolant flow control valves regulate the flow of coolant within the engine and cooling system to optimize temperature management. The market is generally segmented into two main types: 2-way and 3-way valves. </p><p>2-way valves facilitate flow in a single direction, directing coolant either to the engine or radiator. Meanwhile, 3-way valves allow for more versatile routing, enabling simultaneous coolant flow to multiple components. Other variants may include specialized valves, employing unique mechanisms to enhance efficiency or adapt to specific vehicle designs.</p></p>
