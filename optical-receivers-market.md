<p><strong>The Optical Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Digital Hierarchy</li><li>Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical receivers market consists of several key types based on data transmission methodologies. Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (PDH) enables multiple signals to transfer in sync, allowing for efficient bandwidth management. Synchronous Digital Hierarchy (SDH), on the other hand, provides a standardized framework for multiplexing digital signals, enhancing interoperability and network reliability. Synchronous Plesiochronous Digital Hierarchy (SPDH) combines features of both systems, facilitating flexible data rate support while maintaining synchronization across diverse network environments, thus catering to varying transmission requirements.</p></p>
