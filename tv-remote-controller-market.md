<p><p>The TV remote controller market consists of two primary types: traditional TV remote controls and universal remote controllers. Traditional remotes are designed for specific television brands, offering basic functionalities like power, volume, and channel control. In contrast, universal remote controllers can operate multiple devices, including TVs, sound systems, and streaming boxes, providing a more versatile solution for users. This versatility allows consumers to reduce clutter and simplify their entertainment experience by consolidating remotes into a single device.</p></p>
