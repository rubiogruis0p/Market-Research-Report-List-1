<p><ul><li>Single-Wall</li><li>Double-Wall</li><li>Triple-Wall</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated pharmaceutical packaging market consists of three main types: single-wall, double-wall, and triple-wall. Single-wall packaging, made from a single layer of fluted paper, offers lightweight protection for non-hazardous products. Double-wall packaging provides enhanced strength and durability, suitable for heavier or fragile items. Triple-wall packaging features three layers of corrugated material, providing superior protection for sensitive or bulk products during transportation and storage, ensuring they remain secure and intact throughout the supply chain.</p></p>
