<p><p>The spray sunscreen market comprises three primary types: physical, chemical, and hybrid formulations. Physical sunscreens use mineral ingredients like zinc oxide or titanium dioxide to create a barrier that reflects UV rays. Chemical sunscreens contain organic compounds that absorb UV radiation, converting it into heat. Hybrid sunscreens combine both physical and chemical agents, offering broad-spectrum protection. Each type caters to different consumer preferences regarding texture, absorption, and skin compatibility, influencing their selection and market demand.</p></p>
