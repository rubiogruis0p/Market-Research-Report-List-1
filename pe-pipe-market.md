<p><strong>The PE Pipe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) Pipe</li><li>MDPE (Medium -Density Polyethylene) Pipe</li><li>LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyethylene pipe market encompasses three main types: HDPE, MDPE, and LDPE pipes. HDPE pipes are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. MDPE pipes offer a balance of flexibility and strength, suitable for medium-pressure systems, especially in gas distribution. LDPE pipes are characterized by their low density, providing flexibility and ease of installation for low-pressure applications, generally used in irrigation and drainage systems. Each type serves distinct applications based on their physical properties.</p></p>
