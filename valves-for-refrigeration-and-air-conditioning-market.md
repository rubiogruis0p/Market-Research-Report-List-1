<p><p>The refrigeration and air conditioning market features several valve types that control refrigerant flow. Solenoid valves operate electrically to control on/off flow. Globe valves regulate fluid flow with a linear motion, providing precise control. Four-way valves enable the reversal of refrigerant flow in heat pump systems. Electronic expansion valves precisely control refrigerant flow based on temperature and pressure, enhancing system efficiency. Other valves may include check, ball, and pressure relief valves, each serving specific functions in the HVAC system.</p></p>
