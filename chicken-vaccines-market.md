<p><strong>The Chicken Vaccines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Live Vaccines</li><li>Killed Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chicken vaccines market primarily consists of two types: live vaccines and killed vaccines. Live vaccines contain modified but potent pathogens that stimulate a strong immune response, offering long-lasting protection. They are often more effective with fewer doses. In contrast, killed vaccines use inactivated pathogens, ensuring safety without the risk of disease. While they may require multiple doses for adequate immunity, they are typically safer for immunocompromised birds. Both types are crucial for maintaining poultry health and preventing disease outbreaks.</p></p>
