<p><p>The polarization insensitive optical circulator market is primarily categorized into three port and four port types. Three port circulators facilitate the unidirectional flow of light between three optical fibers, ensuring minimal interference from polarization effects. Four port circulators, on the other hand, expand this functionality to four fibers, enabling more complex routing in optical networks. Both types are essential in telecommunications, fiber optic sensors, and data centers, where managing signal integrity and optimizing performance are critical in diverse applications.</p></p>
