<p><p>Diabetic retinopathy treatments include various market types focusing on improving vision and preventing disease progression. Laser surgery uses targeted light to seal leaking blood vessels, reducing vision loss. Corticosteroid injections help reduce inflammation and swelling in the retina. Anti-VEGF drugs inhibit abnormal blood vessel growth, minimizing the risk of complications. Vitrectomy is a surgical procedure that removes the vitreous gel from the eye to address severe cases of retinal damage and bleeding, restoring visual function.</p></p>
