<p><p>Killed steel refers to steel that has been refined to remove oxygen, improving its quality and properties. The two primary types are silicon-killed steel and aluminum-killed steel. Silicon-killed steel uses silicon as a deoxidizer, enhancing strength and conductivity, making it suitable for electrical applications. Aluminum-killed steel employs aluminum for deoxidization, resulting in a fine-grained structure with excellent surface quality, ideal for automotive and construction uses. Both types are vital in manufacturing high-performance steel products.</p></p>
