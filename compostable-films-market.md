<p><p>Compostable films are biodegradable materials designed to break down into organic matter in composting conditions. The rising awareness of environmental issues and strict regulations against single-use plastics have fueled the demand for compostable films. This market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.1% during the forecast period, reflecting significant interest from various sectors, such as food packaging, agriculture, and industrial applications.</p><p>Recent trends indicate an increasing shift towards sustainable packaging solutions driven by consumer preference for environmentally responsible products. Innovations in material science are also enhancing the properties of compostable films, such as strength and barrier performance, making them more appealing for commercial use. Moreover, collaborations between manufacturers and research institutions are further propelling advancements in compostable film technologies. The expanding focus on circular economy initiatives and heightened government support for biodegradable materials are anticipated to support the market's robust growth, positioning compostable films as a critical component of sustainable packaging solutions in the coming years.</p></p>
