<p><p>The ANC (Active Noise Cancelling) headphones market is categorized into three main types: In-Ear, On-Ear, and Over-Ear. In-Ear headphones are compact and fit directly in the ear canal, providing portability and passive noise isolation. On-Ear headphones rest on the outer ear, offering a balance of comfort and sound quality while remaining relatively lightweight. Over-Ear headphones envelop the entire ear, delivering superior noise cancellation and audio fidelity, making them ideal for immersive listening experiences. Each type caters to different user preferences and scenarios.</p></p>
