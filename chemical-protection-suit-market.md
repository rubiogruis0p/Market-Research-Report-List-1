<p><ul><li>Zipper Front</li><li>Zipper Back</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Chemical Protection Suit market can be divided into two primary types based on the zipper placement: Zipper Front and Zipper Back. Zipper Front suits feature a zipper that runs vertically down the front of the suit, allowing for easier donning and doffing, making them suitable for tasks requiring quick access. In contrast, Zipper Back suits have the zipper positioned along the back, enhancing protection and minimizing the risk of chemical exposure during high-risk operations, where integrity is crucial.</p></p>
