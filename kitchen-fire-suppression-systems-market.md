<p><p>Kitchen fire suppression systems are crucial for enhancing safety in commercial kitchens. The market is primarily divided into two types: manual trigger systems and thermal trigger systems. Manual trigger systems require human action to activate, allowing chefs or staff to initiate suppression during a fire. In contrast, thermal trigger systems automatically detect heat and activate suppression without manual intervention, providing rapid response to fires. Both types are essential for preventing fire damage and ensuring safety in food preparation environments.</p></p>
