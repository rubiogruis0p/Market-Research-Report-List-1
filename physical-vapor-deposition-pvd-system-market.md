<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) system market comprises various techniques, including Thermal Evaporation, Sputtering, and Electron Beam Evaporation. Thermal Evaporation involves heating materials to create vapor, which then condenses on a substrate to form thin films. Sputtering uses energetic particles to dislodge atoms from a target material, depositing them onto a surface. Electron Beam Evaporation employs focused electron beams to vaporize materials, allowing for high precision and control in film thickness and composition. Each technique serves diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
