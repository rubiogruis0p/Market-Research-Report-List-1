<p><p>The infrared night-vision scope market is primarily divided into two types: single tube and double tube scopes. Single tube infrared night-vision scopes utilize a compact design that integrates both the objective lens and image intensifier into one unit, offering lightweight and cost-effective solutions for users. In contrast, double tube scopes comprise two separate systems for enhanced depth perception and image clarity, providing a more immersive viewing experience at the expense of added weight and complexity. These options cater to various user preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
