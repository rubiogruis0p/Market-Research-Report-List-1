<p><p>The irrigation cables market features two primary types: single core and multi-core cables. Single core cables consist of a single conductor, ideal for basic irrigation systems with straightforward power needs. On the other hand, multi-core cables contain multiple conductors, making them suitable for complex setups requiring various control functions, such as monitoring or automation. These cable types cater to different irrigation applications, affecting energy efficiency, installation complexity, and overall system performance, thereby influencing market demand and growth.</p></p>
