<p><p>The military servo drive market primarily consists of two types: DC servo drives and AC servo drives. DC servo drives are characterized by their simplicity and high torque at low speeds, making them ideal for precision applications in military systems. In contrast, AC servo drives offer enhanced efficiency, reliability, and performance across a wider range of speeds, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types support critical military operations, including robotics, weapon systems, and automated machinery.</p></p>
