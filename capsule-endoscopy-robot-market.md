<p><p>The capsule endoscopy robot market is primarily divided into two types: Internally Driven Type and Magnetron Type. The Internally Driven Type utilizes an onboard propulsion system, enabling the capsule to navigate through the gastrointestinal tract autonomously, providing a more refined inspection. In contrast, the Magnetron Type employs external magnetic fields to guide the capsule's movement, allowing for precise positioning and control. Both types enhance diagnostic capabilities in gastrointestinal examinations, improving patient outcomes and comfort during procedures.</p></p>
