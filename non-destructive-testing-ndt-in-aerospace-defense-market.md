<p><p>Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) in the Aerospace and Defense market encompasses various methods to evaluate materials and components without causing damage. Ultrasonic Testing utilizes high-frequency sound waves to detect imperfections. Radiography Testing employs X-rays or gamma rays to visualize internal structures. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials through magnetic particles. Eddy-Current Testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect flaws in conductive materials. Other NDT methods include visual inspection, dye penetrant testing, and acoustic emission testing, ensuring reliability and safety.</p></p>
