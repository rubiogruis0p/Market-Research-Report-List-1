<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Steering Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)</li><li>Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)</li><li>Electric Power Steering (EPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive steering device market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS)** relies on hydraulic fluid pressure generated by a pump, providing effective steering assistance but requiring engine power.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Electro-hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS)** combines hydraulic systems with electric motors, enhancing efficiency and reducing engine load while offering variable assistance.</p><p>3. **Electric Power Steering (EPS)** uses electric motors directly for steering, allowing for greater energy efficiency, customizable feedback, and advanced features like lane-keeping assistance, driving towards a more electric future in automotive technology.</p></p>
