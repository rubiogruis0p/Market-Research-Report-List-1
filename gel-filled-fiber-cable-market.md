<p><p>Gel filled fiber cables are designed to protect optical fibers in various environments, utilizing a gel filling that prevents moisture ingress. The market for these cables is segmented into two main types: Single-Mode and Multi-Mode. Single-Mode fibers are optimized for long-distance communication, offering higher bandwidth and lower signal loss by allowing a single light path. In contrast, Multi-Mode fibers are suited for shorter distances, utilizing multiple light paths for data transmission, making them ideal for local area networks and data centers.</p></p>
